Taco Rice

Homepage


Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Red onion
  • Lettuce
  • Neutral oil
  • Shredded cheese
  • Rice
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tomato

Method

  1. Mince the garlic clove and set aside.
  2. Mince the red onion.
  3. Cut the tomato into small cubes and season to make a simple tomato salsa.
  4. Cut the lettuce into thin strips.
  5. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium head. Cook the garlic cloves until fragrant. Than add the red onion and saute until coated with oil.
  6. Add the ground beef and break into small pieces using a spatula.
  7. Add 2 tsp taco seasoning for every 115g.
  8. Serve the steamed rice on a plate or bowl. Put the ground beef on top. Then top with cheese, lettuce and tomato salsa as much as you like.
