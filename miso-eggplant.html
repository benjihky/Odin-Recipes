Miso Eggplant

Homepage


Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Green onion
  • Sesame oil

Miso Sauce

  • 2 tbsp Sake
  • 2 tbsp Mirin
  • 1 tbsp Miso
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce

Method

  1. Cut the eggplant into bite-size pieces. The Japanese "rangiri" cutting technique is reccomended.
  2. Cut the green onions into thin slices
  3. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients for the miso sauce.
  4. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat.
  5. When it's hot, add the sesame oil and distribute in the pan. When the oil is hot, add eggplant.
  6. Cook the eggplants on medium heat, flipping them when the bottom side is brown.
  7. When the eggplants are nicely browned, add the miso sauce and coat them with the sauce by shaking the frying pan.
  8. When the liquid is evaporated, remove the pan from the heat immediately.
  9. Transfer to a serving plate and garnish with green onion.
